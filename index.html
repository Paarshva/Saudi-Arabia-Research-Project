Glossary of Terms

  • NBFI: Non-Bank Financial Institution ‚Äì a licensed lender that is not a deposit-taking bank.
  • BNPL: Buy Now Pay Later ‚Äì short-term instalment financing at point of sale, typically interest-free to the customer.
  • Murabaha: Shariah-compliant cost-plus sale contract.
  • Tawarruq: A Shariah structure allowing cash lending via commodity trades.
  • Sukuk: Islamic bond representing asset ownership or cashflow rights.
  • Securitisation: Packaging receivables into asset-backed securities sold to investors.
  • SAIBOR: Saudi Arabian Interbank Offered Rate (local floating-rate loan benchmark).
  • ECL: Expected Credit Loss ‚Äì IFRS 9 impairment allowance estimate.
  • ROA: Return on Assets ‚Äì net income divided by total assets.
  • ROE: Return on Equity ‚Äì net income divided by equity.
  • PDPL: Saudi Personal Data Protection Law.
